Why we have Well Days

Thanks to things like Droga Yoga and Fresh Cut Fruit in the Kitchen (*powered by Toma), it turns out we're a pretty healthy agency. So even though Droga budgets for sick days, we actually aren't using that many of them. Which means, a ton of them end up going to waste. Until now. If each of us uses just one sick day to volunteer in the community, we can make a huuuuuuge difference. (This is a fact - if you haven't noticed, we're a giant ass agency now.)
